Featured Drinks

Memory of Arashiyama

Arashiyama is a stunningly beautiful district on the outskirts of Kyoto, Japan where flower pedals cover the area like snow on a mountaintop. Arashiyama is Sodoi’s singular coffee, crafted to bring out an amazing floral perfume essence in its aroma under a slightly lighter roast to preserve every nuance of fragrance.

Open Your Heart

Sodoi’s tribute to romance, with beans carefully selected for their natural sweetness and roasted just right, to imbue a rich thick liquor in every cup.

My Dream, My Coffee

Sodoi’s rocket fuel! Heady, strong, with a mix of medium and deep roasts to charge up your batteries and give you that extra boost to grab onto your life and go for it!
